# Nusantara Law Corpus

## Overview
The Nusantara Law Corpus is a comprehensive collection of Indonesian legal reference materials and datasets, designed to support legal research, education, and natural language processing applications.

## Purpose
This repository aggregates authoritative Indonesian legal terminology, constitutional documents, and judicial guidelines to provide:
- Standardized legal terminology definitions
- Constitutional reference materials
- Legal precedents and judicial guidelines
- Multilingual legal glossaries
