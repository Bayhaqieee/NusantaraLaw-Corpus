# Indonesian Legal Glossary (Glosarium Hukum Indonesia)

## Overview
This folder contains a comprehensive collection of Indonesian legal terminology and definitions sourced from the **Mahkamah Agung** (Supreme Court of Indonesia) official legal glossary. The collection provides standardized definitions of legal terms used in Indonesian law across various practice areas including civil procedure, criminal procedure, administrative law, Islamic law (Syariah), intellectual property, and more.

## Source
**Mahkamah Agung Glosarium Hukum**  
Official Website: https://kepaniteraan.mahkamahagung.go.id/glosarium-hukum
